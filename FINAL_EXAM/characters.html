Main Characters

Meet the heroes and villains of Black Clover: Sword of the Wizard King.

Protagonists

Asta

Magicless boy who fights using Anti-Magic swords. Dreams of becoming Wizard King.

Voice Actor: Gakuto Kajiwara

Yuno Grinberryall

Asta’s rival blessed with immense wind and star magic.

Voice Actor: Nobunaga Shimazaki

Noelle Silva

Royal Magic Knight with powerful water spells and Valkyrie armor.

Voice Actor: Kana Yūki

Antagonists

Conrad Leto

Former Wizard King seeking to reshape the Clover Kingdom.

Voice Actor: Toshihiko Seki

Jester Galandros

A resurrected Wizard King who commands illusion and trick-based magic.

Voice Actor: Fumiya Takahashi

Princia Funnybunny

A mighty combat-type Wizard King who overwhelms enemies with brute strength and martial magic.

Voice Actor: Mariya Ise

Edward Avalaché

A former Wizard King mastering ice-based magic with overwhelming defensive power.

Voice Actor: Takayuki Sugo

Damnatio Kira

High-ranking judge who manipulates Scale Magic to control and punish others.

Voice Actor: Ryōtarō Okiayu
