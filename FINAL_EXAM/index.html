Black Clover: Sword of the Wizard King

Asta and the Magic Knights battle resurrected Wizard Kings in a war that threatens the Clover Kingdom.

Movie Highlights

  • Legendary Wizard Kings return from the past
  • Asta unleashes his full anti-magic power
  • Yuno’s Spirit Dive evolution

Synopsis

In a world where magic defines status, Asta—born without magic—aims to become the Wizard King. When former Wizard Kings are revived to destroy the Clover Kingdom, the Magic Knights must unite and surpass their limits to protect everything they love.
